Step 1.

Build/manage your budget by clicking the Manage Budget button on the home screen.


Step 2.

Scroll through the categories and enter values into the text fields that apply to you.


Step 3.

Hit the file button to save your entries and be taken to your budget summary. Here you can review your input and suggested savings amount.


Step 4.

Click on the toolbars main menu and go to Settings. Enter your information to receive text messages if you mistakenly go out of budget.


Step 5.

Go to your widgets and click and drag Money Educations to your home screen. This enables you to continually monitor your savings. Click the widget anytime to be taken to your budget summary.


Step 6.

In order to share your budget, both parties click the Share Budget button and align their phones back to back. Once prompt just click to accept the information sent. This option uses your phones NFC capabilities to beam information to another user's phone.

In order to use this feature please insure that your phones NFC features are enabled.

You will also need to enable your Androids Beaming capabilities.
